what is Nextun?

Nextun is a software solution that helps organizations make informed decisions by providing an interface to analyze and understand data trends and patterns. It integrates data from multiple sources, offers a web platform for analysis, and provides visual representation of data, so users can easily observe and understand what's happening in their business and make informed decisions about their next move.
